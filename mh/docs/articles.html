Articles and websites about MisterHouse

  • Paul Chamberlain set up a TWiki web-based collaboration site for user-supplied documentation.
  • Ron Klinkien has a 'MisterHouse from Scratch' weblog site with a nice collection of links, resources, notes and commentary.
  • Thomas Vogl has a 'Misterhouse Journal' where he cronicles his experiences of setting up MisterHouse on Linux Redhat.
  • Pete Flaherty has a MrAudrey website with a mh compatable Audrey image, along with useful Audrey related notes.
  • Linuxworld.com article from October 99.
  • Circuit Cellar Ink article from October 99.
  • Brian's page has a nice article on "The MisterHouse APRS Tracking System (MHATS)", using MisterHouse and Ham radios to track vehicles.
  • The Perl Journal article from Spring 00.
  • The 10/00 Wired Magazine has an article about the Perl Whirl, a geekcruise.com trip up to Alaska. It doesn't have that much to do with MisterHouse, but it was a fun trip and it is an interesting article about Perl authors and users.
  • NewsForge mentioned MisterHouse in an article debating open source programing on Windows.
  • Slashdot had a post on MisterHouse on May 2003.
